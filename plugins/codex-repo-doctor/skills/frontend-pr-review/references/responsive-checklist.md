# Responsive Checklist

Check at minimum:

- 375px mobile
- 768px tablet
- 1024px laptop
- 1440px desktop

Look for:

- Header or mobile navigation overflow.
- Cards, grids, and tables breaking at narrow widths.
- Modals, drawers, and popovers exceeding viewport boundaries.
- Buttons or touch targets becoming too small.
- Text clipping, wrapping badly, or overlapping nearby content.
- Images losing aspect ratio or hiding important subject matter.
